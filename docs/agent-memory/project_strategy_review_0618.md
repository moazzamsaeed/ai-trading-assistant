**THE CORE INSIGHT (the differentiator):** our trend/LLM strategies BOUGHT options → paid the VRP + needed to predict DIRECTION (no edge, proven coin-flip). The iron condor SELLS options → collects the VRP + needs NO direction (profit from theta + implied>realized). **HIGH-RISK VARIANTS (same VRP principle, more risk = more credit/return), grounded in research just pulled:**
- **Short strangle** (sell OTM put + call, NO wings) — 2 legs not 4 (half the cost that killed the condor!), keeps full credit, **~70% PoP** (vs condor ~60%), but **undefined tail risk**. Manage the tail with a **mechanical STOP + sizing** instead of paying for wings (= self-insure, suits high risk tolerance).
- **Short straddle** (sell ATM) — max credit; tastytrade: keep **40-50% of daily theta** on ATM SPY straddles vs **25% on 16-delta strangles** → ATM/tighter = more premium captured but tighter win zone.
- **1-1-2 ratio** (buy 1 put, sell 2 puts) — net short premium w/ downside tail; net-negative-vega; assignment/margin risk.
- **THE KILLER RISK for all: 0DTE gamma near expiry** — a sudden move sends shorts ITM fast, gamma spikes → must have a hard stop, never oversize ("one rogue move destroys the account if you oversize").
